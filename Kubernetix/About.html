About Kubernetix

Kubernetix is a leading electronics repair shop based in South Africa, offering a wide range of electronic services to engineers and individuals. Our services include system manufacturing, hardware development, programming (processors), LCD application design, industrial system repairs, onsite diagnostic support, and general technical support. We take pride in standing out from our competitors by delivering top-notch technical solutions to our clients. Our team is passionate about providing high-quality services to meet the unique needs of our customers.
